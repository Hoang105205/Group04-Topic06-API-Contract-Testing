During my research on Postbot using the EShop SUT system, I used Claude to help plan my work, guide me through specific steps in Postman, analyze real test results (including two bugs I discovered: an incorrect percentage discount calculation and a missing coupon usage-limit check), and draft content for Section 5.3 and Section 6 of the User Guide, as well as the draft script for my introduction video.
Strengths: Claude explained technical concepts clearly and in an easy-to-understand way, with concrete examples. The day-by-day, step-by-step planning was very detailed, which kept me from getting lost in a large workload. It was especially helpful when Claude helped me turn raw findings into well-structured "Failure Modes" (description – example – consequence – prevention), transforming raw data into usable report content.
Areas for improvement: In the early stages, Claude gave guidance based on general assumptions rather than reading the actual repository directly, which led to some information (endpoints, sample payloads) not fully matching the real system, requiring mid-course corrections. Some early instructions were also fairly long-winded and repeated similar steps, which made it harder for me to pinpoint exactly what to do next. Once real data from the repo was incorporated (via web search), accuracy improved significantly.
